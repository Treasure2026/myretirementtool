Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

Does my financial data go to your servers?
No. All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We never see, collect, or store your financial numbers. You can export your plan as a JSON file anytime for backup.
Is this financial advice?
No — these are planning tools for your own use. The outputs are based on assumptions you enter and are hypothetical projections. Always consult a licensed financial advisor for personalized advice before making major decisions.
What happens after I buy?
You'll receive your license key and email address by email within minutes. Open the app in any browser, enter your key, and you're in. Works on Mac, Windows, iPad, and mobile.
How many devices can I use it on?
One license = one installation. If you need to move it to a new device, contact us and we'll sort it out. Family/advisor multi-seat licenses are available.
Do I need to know anything about finance to use this?
No. The tools come pre-filled with reasonable default assumptions. You just update the numbers that apply to you — your salary, SS amount, IRA balance, expenses. Everything else calculates automatically.
I'm a financial advisor. Can I use this with clients?
Yes, with an advisor license. Contact us at support@myretirementtool.com for pricing on multi-client, white-label, and advisor packages.
